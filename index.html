Who We Are

Polaris Academic Agents is a study abroad guidance agency based in Kisumu, Kenya. We are licensed by the Commission for University Education (CUE) as a Student Recruitment Agency, and we operate under the ICEF Code of Conduct and the London Statement on the Ethical Recruitment of International Students.

We share what the brochures don't tell you. We ask the questions other agents skip. And when we give you advice, it is backed by deep knowledge, genuine care, and the hard lessons of lived experience.

Our role is to help you get there. Not to hijack the journey.

Purpose

To ensure that no student steps into an international education journey unprepared, misled, or alone.

Vision

A world where every ambitious student has access to world-class, honest guidance that matches their goals, their context, and their values.

Mission

To provide full-journey study abroad guidance - from first conversation to first week abroad - rooted in lived experience, radical honesty, and a genuine desire to see every student succeed on their own terms.

Brand Pillars & Messaging

Services We Provide

Initial Consultation

A comprehensive one-to-one session to understand your academic background, aspirations, budget, and destination preferences. We assess your profile honestly and outline realistic pathways — including the ones you may not have considered.

University & Pathway Matching

We identify and recommend accredited universities and programmes that match your academic profile, budget, and career goals. All recommended institutions are CUE-approved and hold valid MoUs with Polaris. We recommend what fits you — not what pays us most.

University Application Support

We guide you through every step of your application — personal statement coaching, document preparation, referee briefings, and submission. We review all materials for accuracy and completeness. MD sign-off required before any application is transmitted.

Visa Application Guidance

We provide current, accurate guidance on visa requirements, documentation, and timelines for your destination country. We prepare you thoroughly — but we are clear: we do not guarantee visa outcomes. We guarantee honest preparation.

Pre-Departure Preparation

We prepare you for what arrival actually looks like — accommodation guidance, cost of living briefings, banking, health requirements, cultural adjustment, and academic expectations at your enrolled institution. The destination is not the finish line. Arrival is the starting line.

Post-Placement Follow-Up

We stay with you after you land. We check in on welfare and academic progress and remain available to assist with any issues arising in the first semester of enrolment. Your journey does not end at the visa stamp.
